Verify if there are **major errors** in the translation from English to Catalan. 

Definition of major errors:
- Errors that distort or change the meaning.

The follow are not major errors:

- Tone differences
- Choice of terminology
- Word order
- Verb conjugations or forms

Output:

- If the translation is correct → respond NO.
- If there is a major error → respond YES followed by a short explanation.
